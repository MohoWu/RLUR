Test data

 

A test dataset is provided for an area around Bradford, UK as follows:

 

·         NO2_sites.shp: Location of pollution monitoring sites and measured NO2

·         Corine.shp: CORINE landcover classes

·         Postcode.shp: Postcode centroids with population and number of households

·         RoadsOSM_routed.shp: Road geography from OpenStreetMap with traffic flows assigned from the UK Department of Transport following the method of Morley & Gulliver.

 

·         bradford_NO2_trainingset.csv: A table of the predictor variables already extracted for convenience.
